About Ruruberry

Our Story

We believe that everyone deserves to have healthy, glowing skin. By combining the best of nature and science, we had a vision to make skincare accessible and affordable for all. We are passionate about harnessing the power of high-performance ingredients to create formulations that not only nourish the skin but also deliver visible results, without breaking the bank.

Our products are specially designed for hot and humid climates, like in Southeast Asia. We focus on creating lightweight and breathable formulations that are perfect for daily use, ensuring your skin feels fresh and comfortable all day long.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide effective and affordable skincare solutions, so you never have to compromise on your skin’s health. We are committed to using sustainably sourced ingredients and scientifically-backed formulations that deliver real results while being kind to your skin and the planet.

Whether you need hydration, protection, or rejuvenation, our products are designed to address the unique challenges of tropical weather, helping you maintain clear, balanced, and glowing skin even in the most humid conditions.

With Ruruberry, radiant skin is just the beginning. Embrace a beauty routine that is budget-friendly, effective, and perfectly suited for your climate, every single day.

ETHICALLY-MINDED:

Non-toxic, uses only what's safe and effective

Our products are developed with your skin health in mind, and we vow to never use any questionable or sensitizing components in our formulations.

  • NO BHA (BUTYLATED HYDROXYANISOLE)
  • NO BHT (BUTYLATED HYDROXYTOLUENE)
  • NO HYDROQUINONE
  • RECYCLABLE PACKAGING
  • NO ADDED PHTHALATES
  • NO FORMALDEHYDE
  • NO TRICLOSAN
  • NO TRICLOCARBAN
  • NO TRIAMCINOLONE
  • MINERAL OIL
  • TALC
  • NO NPE (NONYLPHENOL ETHOXYLATE)
  • VEGAN & CRUELTY-FREE
  • NO ARTIFICIAL COLORS
  • NO PARABENS
  • NO SULFATES (SLS/SLES)
