What am i

A lightweight, power-packed serum that combats dark spots, enlarged pores, inflammatory acne and free radicals with precision.

Why pick me

Niacinamide, a form of Vitamin B3, is a well-researched and proven multidimensional ingredient that can effectively regulate pigment-making cells, normalize sebum production, tighten lax pores, smooth rough edges and visibly brighten tone. This water-soluble vitamin also works synergistically with the natural substances in your skin to maintain a healthy barrier, and stands out because of its versatility for almost any skin care and skin type. Additionally enhanced with Hyaluronic Acid, a hydrating agent known for its ability to attract and hold onto 1000x its weight in moisture, to help plump away wrinkles and firm up sagging skin. Further supported by Sodium Ascorbyl Phosphate, a hydrophilic derivative of Ascorbic Acid that heals blemishes while also being gentler and more stable, and Panthenol, a provitamin of B5 that relieves the symptoms of irritated skin.

Key Ingredients

Niacinamide: A vitamin that brightens skin, reduces the appearance of blemishes, and balances sebum activity. Zinc PCA: A mineral that helps to control oil production and reduce the look of enlarged pores.

Good to Know

It is actually perfectly safe and even beneficial to mix both Niacinamide and Sodium Ascorbyl Phosphate, either together in the same product or combined from layering different products. The worries about rendering them ineffective, resulting in a dreaded yellow solution, are fairly unjustified as that is merely a product of a harmless charge-transfer interaction; while they can in certain condition create Niacin, leading to a possible flush, that reaction will only occur under extreme heat over extended periods of time.
